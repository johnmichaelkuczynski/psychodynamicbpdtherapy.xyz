## Description (copy-paste)

Psychodynamic Treatment of BPD 101 is a self-paced, plain-language "baby course" on the psychodynamic treatment of borderline personality disorder — the meaning beneath the instability of emotion, identity, and relationships. It's taught, tutored, drilled, and graded entirely by AI, and it grades reasoning on an **inverted scale**: most "critical thinking" rewards caution, but here hedging is the failure mode.

The boldest, most-falsifiable interpretation the clinical evidence supports about the hidden function beneath a symptom or behavior earns top credit. The cautious dodge ("everyone has mood swings," "you can't really know what's inside someone") earns near-zero — and so does a lurid overreach the evidence defeats ("she's just manipulative"). Every answer has to commit to the strongest honest reading AND name the cheapest observation that would prove it wrong.

This walkthrough shows the real app end to end: the eight-section curriculum, lessons you can read at three depths, a section-scoped AI tutor that streams answers grounded in the exact paragraph you're reading, one homework per section in your choice of format (multiple choice, hybrid, or written) with a single-attempt lock, inverted partial-credit grading with a written rationale on every item, adaptive practice that ramps difficulty, a four-phase reasoning diagnostic, two-layer AI-authorship detection, and live analytics.

### What you'll see
- 📚 An 8-section, one-unit curriculum on the psychodynamic treatment of BPD
- 🧠 Lessons at three depths (Short / Medium / Long), AI-rewritten on demand
- 💬 A section-scoped AI tutor that streams answers grounded in your reading
- 📝 One homework per section in your format: MCQ, Hybrid, or Written — single attempt, locks on submit
- ⚖️ Inverted partial-credit grading that rewards the strongest falsifiable read and punishes hedging, with a written rationale
- 🔁 Adaptive practice that moves difficulty up after a streak and down after a miss
- 📈 A four-phase reasoning diagnostic that tracks growth across the course
- 🛡️ Two-layer AI-authorship detection: a text classifier (GPTZero) plus a keystroke-pattern detector
- 📊 Live analytics — attempts, accuracy, streak, and per-section mastery

### The 8 sections
1. Understanding BPD: Emotion, Identity, and Unstable Relationships
2. Splitting and the Black-and-White World
3. Object Relations: How Early Bonds Become Inner Templates
4. Identity Diffusion: The Unstable Sense of Self
5. Mentalization: Learning to Read Minds, Including One's Own (MBT)
6. Transference-Focused Psychotherapy: Healing Through the Relationship (TFP)
7. The Evidence: Where Psychodynamic Treatment Genuinely Shines
8. A Treatment Arc, Start to Finish
