## Description (copy-paste)

A complete, one-unit course on the psychodynamic treatment of borderline personality disorder, delivered across eight structured sections — from understanding the instability of emotion, identity, and relationships, through splitting, object relations, identity diffusion, mentalization (MBT), and transference-focused psychotherapy (TFP), to the clinical evidence and a full treatment arc from start to finish. Every section ships with a full lesson and a graded homework, and the entire course is taught, tutored, drilled, and graded by AI.

The curriculum is depth-oriented: it treats the instability of emotion, identity, and relationships as carrying meaning — how overwhelming affect, splitting, and an unstable self-image can be read, how impulsive behavior works to regulate unbearable feeling and ward off abandonment, and how reading the conflict beneath the behavior complements evidence-based treatments like DBT. Each lesson can be read at three depths (Short, Medium, Long), and a section-scoped AI tutor streams answers grounded in the exact paragraph you are reading.

Grading runs on an **inverted scale**. Most "critical thinking" rewards caution; here, hedging is the failure mode. The strongest, most-falsifiable interpretation the clinical evidence supports about the hidden function beneath a symptom or behavior earns top credit. The cautious dodge ("everyone has mood swings," "you can't really know what's inside someone") earns near-zero — and so does a lurid overreach the evidence defeats ("she's just manipulative"). Every answer has to commit to the strongest honest reading and name the cheapest observation that would prove it wrong. Every graded item returns a written rationale.

This walkthrough shows the real application end to end: the eight-section curriculum, three-depth lessons, the streaming section-scoped AI tutor, one homework per section in your choice of format (multiple choice, hybrid, or written) with a single-attempt lock, inverted partial-credit grading, adaptive practice that ramps difficulty, a four-phase reasoning diagnostic, two-layer AI-authorship detection, and live analytics.
